Tradeit is a P2P trading platform.

You can become a seller by creating a post, providing information about the items they are willing to sell.

You can also become a buyer by browsing other sellers' posts to find the items they want to buy.



Technology Stack of Tradeit:

Java(J2EE), HTML, JavaScript, AJAX, JQuery, MySQL
